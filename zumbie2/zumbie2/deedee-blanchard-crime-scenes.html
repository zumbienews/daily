    One of the most important aspects of CSI is the collection of evidence. This can include anything from fingerprints and DNA to weapons and clothing. Evidence collection must be done carefully and thoroughly, as any mistakes could jeopardize the case.

  • Reconstruction of events

    Once the evidence has been collected, CSI investigators will attempt to reconstruct the events of the crime. This can be done by examining the physical evidence, as well as by interviewing witnesses.
