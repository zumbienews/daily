Who is Sophia Mudd?

Sophia Mudd is an American social media influencer, model, and entrepreneur. She is best known for her popular TikTok account, where she has over 6 million followers. Mudd's content primarily consists of dance videos, lip-syncing videos, and comedy sketches.

Mudd was born in Los Angeles, California, on September 20, 2003. She began posting videos on TikTok in 2019, and quickly gained a following for her engaging and entertaining content. In addition to her TikTok account, Mudd also has a popular Instagram account, where she has over 1 million followers. She has used her platform to promote various brands and products, and has also launched her own line of merchandise.

Mudd is known for her positive and upbeat personality, and she often uses her platform to spread messages of body positivity and self-acceptance. She is also an advocate for mental health awareness, and has spoken out about her own struggles with anxiety and depression.

Sophia Mudd is a role model for many young people, and she continues to inspire and entertain her followers with her positive attitude and engaging content.
